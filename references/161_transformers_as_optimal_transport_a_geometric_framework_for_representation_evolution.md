Transformers as Optimal Transport: A Geometric Framework for Representation Evolution
=====================================================================================

Primary link:
- https://openreview.net/forum?id=IzAooxm1yv

Why this is in the reference pack:
- It provides a principled OT framing for representation transport rather than
  treating head matching as a pure heuristic.
- It supports the next heavier method class: Sinkhorn or Wasserstein-style soft
  transport across non-canonical head spaces.
