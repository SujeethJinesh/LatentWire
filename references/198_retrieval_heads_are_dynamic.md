# Retrieval Heads are Dynamic

- URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11162
- Date: 2026-02-17
- Why it matters here:
  - argues retrieval-head behavior is timestep-specific rather than fixed
  - supports our blocker read that averaged calibration-time templates are likely too static
  - motivates moving query-conditioning into the live transport or routing decision instead of another mean-template branch
