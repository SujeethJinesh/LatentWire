# MoRA: On-the-Fly Low-Rank Adaptation

- Date: 2025-10-14
- Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.12245
- Why it matters here: points to dynamically generated low-rank weights as a bridge/projector mechanism, which is more expressive than the current static `bridge_ridge`.
- Most useful takeaway: if the bridge lane survives, the next meaningful step is likely query-conditioned low-rank adaptation rather than another fixed linear bridge.
