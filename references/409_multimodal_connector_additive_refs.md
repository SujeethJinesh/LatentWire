# Multimodal Connector Additive Refs (2026-04-21)

Purpose: bank the strongest multimodal / connector / staged-repair ideas that
could transfer into LatentWire as additive branches rather than paper pivots.

## Strongest Sources

1. Dense Connector for MLLMs
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.13800
   Why it matters: strongest multi-layer connector reference for richer bridge
   taps without a full architectural rewrite.

2. Libra
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.10140
   Why it matters: clean decoupling between within-modality modeling and
   cross-modality bridging.

3. Prompt-Aware Adapter
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.15684
   Why it matters: best prompt-conditioned token/adapter reference for making
   bridge output route- or task-aware.

4. LLaVA-Mini
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.03895
   Why it matters: strongest aggressive token-compression reference for a
   summary-plus-residual sidecar experiment.

5. LEO-MINI
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.04653
   Why it matters: routed multi-expert conditioning is a better analogue than
   naive confidence-only bridge routing.

6. v1: Learning to Point Visual Tokens for Multimodal Grounded Reasoning
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.18842
   Why it matters: selective revisitation is a strong template for one-step
   source re-fetch when the bridge is uncertain.

7. Bridge: Growing Visual Generative Capacity for Pre-Trained MLLMs
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.01546
   Why it matters: coarse semantic channel plus fine residual refinement maps
   well to a staged communication story.

8. MDPO
   Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.11839
   Why it matters: explicit conditional preference optimization is a useful
   template for source-conditioned repair.

## Exact Next Ablations

1. prompt-aware adaptive bridge on top of the live dynalign lane
2. routed repair expert instead of one monolithic residual branch
3. selective re-fetch / revisit when the bridge is uncertain
4. coarse semantic summary plus tiny residual sidecar

## Current Read

- These are additive connector ideas, not replacements for the live real lane.
- The transferable lesson is staged or routed repair, not simply adding more
  auxiliary tokens.
