# MASSV

- Title: MASSV: Multimodal Adaptation and Self-Data Distillation for Speculative Decoding of Vision-Language Models
- Date: 2025-05-15
- Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.10526

Why this is in the backlog:

- Strong lightweight-projector reference for adapting between latent spaces
  without training a full end-to-end bridge.
- Useful for the current cross-model KV setting because it combines a small
  bridge with self-distillation, which is a plausible next step if static
  transport plus tiny correction remains unstable.
